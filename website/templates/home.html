About UtilityBuddy

“I struggled with high utilities bills, but my provider didn’t provide enough data for me to understand my usage! I thought there had to be a better way…”

- Calum Evans, 2020

That’s when UtilityBuddy™ was formed. In August 2021, from Calum’s humble living room, the team wirelessly received data from their first utility meter. In this ground-breaking moment, the team knew that they were onto something revolutionary.

How it Works

1. Plug in the UtilityBuddy™ to power with the provided USB-C cable. Connect the device to your Wi-Fi network.

2. Add the meter IDs of the meters you would like to monitor. These are 9-digit numbers found on the top of Itron gas, water, and electricity meters.

3. Sit back and watch your utilities usage with ease. You will see real-time utilities data, with this you’ll be notified of high-usage, bill estimation, leak detection, and more! New features continually being rolled out.
